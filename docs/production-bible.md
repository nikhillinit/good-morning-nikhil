# Good Morning, Nikhil: Production Bible

## 1. Story Development

### Logline
A weary focus-tester (the user) is abducted by erratic TV hosts Steve Harvey and Jeff Goldblum and forced to survive a gauntlet of volatile reality show segments to determine if their "friend" Nikhil gets renewed for another season of life.

### Treatment
The experience is a meta-parody. The user is cast as the **Lead Focus Tester**, the only person whose opinion can save (or doom) the pilot episode of "Nikhil". Steve and Jeff break the fourth wall to address the user directly, desperately trying to extract "good television" and "raw data" from them. As they progress through the channels (Feud, Bachelor, Shark Tank), the stakes get more personal and the facade of the TV shows begins to strip away, culminating in a raw confessional (Survivor, Maury) and the final Control Room verdict where the user must give their director's notes. 

### Beat Sheet
1. **The Setup (Cold Open/Welcome):** Steve and Jeff establish the stakes. Nikhil's pilot is testing poorly. They need the user's raw, unfiltered opinions to save the show. 
2. **The Qualifier (Relationship):** The user's credibility is established. 
3. **The Quickfire (Family Feud):** Surface-level judgments. The hosts demand fast, punchy TV content.
4. **The Commodification (Commercial Break):** Nikhil is boiled down to a product by the network.
5. **The Culling (Bachelor):** The user is forced to eliminate Nikhil's weaker traits to "save the rose data."
6. **The Valuation (Shark Tank):** The user is pitched on the value of Nikhil's mind.
7. **The Confessional (Survivor):** The flashy studio lights turn off. It's just the user and a camera. The emotional climax where the truth comes out. 
8. **The Contrast (Maury):** Steve demands the dark truth. The narrative whiplash of what is projected vs. what is real.
9. **The Verdict (Control Room):** The user is handed the director's microphone. What is the ultimate correction?
10. **The Fade Out (Credits):** The data ends. Did the user save the show? 

## 2. Character Creation

### The User (Protagonist)
- **Role:** The Lead Focus Tester / Guest Critic.
- **Objective:** Survive the barrage of TV segments and submit their honest opinions of Nikhil.

### Steve Harvey (Co-Host / The Taskmaster)
- **Role:** The desperate network professional trying to keep the show together.
- **Voice:** High-energy, exasperated, demanding, speaks directly to the user to push them forward.

### Jeff Goldblum (Co-Host / The Chaos Agent)
- **Role:** The erratic celebrity hired for star power who doesn't understand what show he's on.
- **Voice:** Rambling, philosophical, easily distracted.
